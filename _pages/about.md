I am a postdoctoral research associate in the Department of Computer Science at [Duke University](https://cs.duke.edu) working with [Prof. Michael Reiter](https://reitermk.github.io/). I obtained my PhD from [Stony Brook University](https://www.cs.stonybrook.edu) where I was advised by [Prof. Radu Sion](https://zxr.io/). Before that I obtained my undergraduate degree in Computer Science from [Jadavpur University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jadavpur_University), India

I build systems that incorporate cryptographic protocols in efficient, real-world settings. My research lies in the intersection of cryptography, systems and theory. 
Topics that I have worked on include secure databases and cloud computing technologies, secure end-to-end messaging, and embedded hardware. I also have casual interest in several aspects of machine learning and computational geometry. 


I will be joining [University of Illinois at Chicago](https://cs.uic.edu) as an assistant professor starting in Fall 2023.
